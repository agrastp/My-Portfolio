About Me

Hi, I'm Pamela Agrast, a full-stack enginer living in Columbus, Ohio. After working in the Early Education industry for 15 years, I wanted to broaden my skills and dive deeper into the ever-growing field of tecnology.

In May 2023, I left the comfort of my career to pursue my passion in developing and creating online content. My career as an educator developed my professional skills in management, organization, project development, and as a prominent team member.

Along with technology, my passions are creating art, edcuation advocacy, and volunteering at a therapy horse farm that aids veterans, first-responders, and community members who need extra therapy support.
